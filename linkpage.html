Bars

Summer Terrace

The ‘Summer Terrace’, as you would have probably guessed, is live all Summer long, with concerts, stand ups, DJs and a young crowd here every night. Just for a few euros you can enjoy live music, Lithuanian beer and good vibes

Alchemikas

If you are more of a cocktail kind of person, we’ve got something for you too! The bar ‘Alchemist’ was announced the city’s best cocktail bar by the prestigious VMG Culinary Awards. With a vast collection of rare spirits from all over the world, this coctail lab will leave you'll be spoilt for choice

Alaus Biblioteka

The ‘Beer Library’ is like no other pub you’ve seen. It looks and feels like a real library, with high ceilings and shelves stacked with books (and beers). The idea is to encourage you to not only just beer, but to also consciously appreciate the taste and the variety on offer
